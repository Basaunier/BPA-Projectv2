Our Mission

Our goal is to prevent the spread of any further greenhouse gases and to show the future generations (and the present) that our world is slowly falling apart and that we can change these actions little by little with every person that follows this idea. Not only that it's a much healthier way of living and it helps our world stay as clean as possible! The main contributors to these gases are food, consumption, transportation, and household energy. All of these contributors come from individuals and groups such as schools, companies, and industries. The problems of our generation are coming sooner than we think and we must help other people acknowledge how close it is.
